Our Story

Taqat is a collective of freelancers, entrepreneurs, and dreamers whose careers were affected by the ongoing war in Gaza. Through displacement, hunger, and loss, we sought to find a glimpse of hope, an opportunity to help Gazan talent rise together from the suffering and rebuild brick by brick.
We began as a simple initiative that offered free access to stable internet and power for freelancers and remote workers displaced from their homes. Today, we have 400+ members and counting, and we have a thriving community that offers networking opportunities, job opportunities, and support for hardworking individuals from all backgrounds.

Our Message

At Taqat, we envision a thriving community where freelancers, remote workers, and entrepreneurs don’t just survive—they thrive.
Vision We see a future where talent is no longer limited by geography, where skills lead to global opportunities, and where every individual is empowered to build a meaningful, sustainable career from right here in Gaza.
Mission Together with our success partners, we engage in community events and surround our freelancers with the tools, training programs, and support system they need to grow beyond their own expectations.
